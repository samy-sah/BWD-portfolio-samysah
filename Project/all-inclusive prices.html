FACTORS DEFINED BY THE BRUSSELS-CAPITAL REGION COME INTO PLAY WHEN CALCULATING THE PRICE OF YOUR TRIP :

  • Pick-up: 2,40€.
  • Tariff I (in the 19 communes of the Brussels-Capital Region): 1.80€/km
  • Tariff II (outside the 19 communes) : 2,70€/km
  • Waiting time: €30.00 per hour (the taximeter automatically switches to the waiting time when the taxi is travelling at a speed of less than 19km/h, e.g. in traffic jams, at traffic lights, etc.).
  • Between 10 p.m. and 6 a.m., a flat-rate supplement of 2.00€ is charged to the customer (the taximeter is equipped with a calendar clock which allows the automatic recording of this flat-rate supplement).
  • There is no extra charge for luggage or a ride to the airport.
